# How to Use

## 1. Select a Category

On the homepage, click on any category card (Champions, Skins, Abilities, etc.)

## 2. Configure Options

Chose your preferred display type, champion scope and additional options based on the category.

## 3. Create Your Tierlist

- **Drag and drop** images to organize them into tiers
- **Double-click** an image to return it to the bottom pool
- **Click tier settings** (gear icon) to customize colors and labels
- **Add/remove tiers** by clicking on the title or using tier controls
- **Reorder tiers** using the up/down arrows
- **Hover over images** for 1-2 seconds to see a larger preview

## 4. Export Your Tier List

Click the "Download Tierlist" button to save your tierlist as a PNG image
